Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. Each is assigned a random number between 1 and 12 at the start of the game.

The values of each crystal are hidden from you. Each time you click a crystal its value is added to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the given random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

Each time a new round starts, the random number and values of each crystal will be reset.
